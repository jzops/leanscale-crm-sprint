## Executive Summary

LeanScale is launching a productized CRM implementation offering targeting B2B tech companies. The campaign positions LeanScale as the fast, outcome-guaranteed alternative to traditional consulting firms.

**Core Positioning:** "Your CRM should take less time to implement than your free trial takes to expire."

---

## Market Context

### The Problem

| Pain Point | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| CRM implementations take 6-18 months | Startups can't wait that long between funding rounds |
| 30-50% of implementations fail to meet expectations | Burned buyers are skeptical of all vendors |
| Big consultants front-load discovery, delay execution | Founders pay for PowerPoints, not pipelines |
| "Live" doesn't mean "adopted" | Systems get built but reps don't use them |

### The Buyer

**Primary:** Series A-C B2B tech companies
- Headcount: 50-500
- Recently closed funding, need to scale GTM fast
- Current CRM is either nonexistent, broken, or being outgrown
- Decision makers: CRO, VP Sales, Head of RevOps, sometimes CEO

**Secondary:** PE/VC portfolio ops teams looking for a go-to partner

### Why Now

- 91% of companies with 11+ employees use CRM
- Cloud CRM adoption at 87% and growing
- Mobile CRM users hit sales quotas at 3x the rate of non-mobile users
- AI is changing what's possible in CRM automation
- Economic pressure means tighter timelines, smaller budgets

## Product Offerings

### Offer 1: 30-Day CRM Implementation Sprint

**Price:** $25,000 flat

**What's Included:**
- HubSpot or Salesforce setup (client's choice)
- Pipeline stages, deal properties, lead scoring
- Lead routing and assignment rules
- Core integrations (calendar, email, Slack)
- Sales reporting dashboard (pipeline, velocity, conversion)
- Team training (2 sessions)
- 30 days of Slack support post-launch

**Guarantee:** If your team isn't actively using the CRM by Day 30, we keep working at no additional cost until they are.

**Ideal For:**
- Companies with no CRM or a barely-used one
- Post-funding teams scaling sales headcount
- Founders who've been "doing it in spreadsheets"

---

### Offer 2: 60-Day CRM Migration

**Price:** $35,000 flat

**What's Included:**
- Full data migration (contacts, companies, deals, activities, notes)
- Custom object mapping and recreation
- Workflow/automation rebuild in new system
- Integration reconnection
- Historical reporting preservation
- Team retraining on new platform
- Parallel run period (old system stays live until new one is proven)
- 30 days of Slack support post-launch

**Guarantee:** Zero data loss. Zero downtime. If we miss either, we fix it free.

**Ideal For:**
- Salesforce → HubSpot migrations (most common)
- HubSpot → Salesforce migrations (scaling up)
- Legacy CRM → modern platform moves
- Post-acquisition system consolidation

---

### Offer 3: The CRM Guarantee (Premium)

**Price:** $30,000 (refundable)

**What's Included:**
- Everything in the 30-Day Sprint
- Plus: 30-day adoption monitoring period
- Daily login tracking, activity monitoring
- Adoption coaching for lagging reps
- Weekly adoption reports to leadership

**Guarantee:** 80%+ daily active usage by sales team within 60 days, or full refund.

**Ideal For:**
- Companies who've been burned before
- Skeptical buyers who need proof
- Leadership teams struggling with rep adoption
