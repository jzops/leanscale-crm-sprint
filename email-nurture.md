**Body:**

### Email 4: Day 10

**Subject:** HubSpot or Salesforce?

**Body:**

One of the most common questions we get:

"Should we use HubSpot or Salesforce?"

Short answer: It depends.

**Choose HubSpot if:**
- Your sales team is <30 people
- You want fast implementation
- You're budget-conscious
- Marketing alignment is a priority
- You don't need complex CPQ or territory management

**Choose Salesforce if:**
- You're scaling past 50 reps
- You have complex sales processes
- Enterprise integrations matter (NetSuite, heavy CPQ)
- You're planning to IPO (investors sometimes have opinions)

**The real truth:**
Both work fine for most B2B companies. The CRM matters less than how it's implemented.

A clean HubSpot beats a messy Salesforce every time.

We do both. If you're not sure which is right, we can talk through it.

## Email Best Practices

**Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 40 characters
- Lowercase, conversational
- Questions work well
- Avoid "CRM Implementation" — too formal

**Body:**
- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences max)
- One idea per email
- Always include clear CTA
- Use "Joe" not "The LeanScale Team"

**Timing:**
- Send 8-9 AM recipient timezone
- Weekdays only
- Respect unsubscribes immediately
