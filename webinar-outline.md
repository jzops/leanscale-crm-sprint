# CRM Sprint Webinar Series

## Concept: "The 30-Day CRM Sprint: Live Build"

**Format:** 4-part series, 1 hour each, live every Thursday for 4 weeks
**Platform:** Zoom Webinar (record for evergreen content)
**Promotion:** LinkedIn, email list, partner channels

---

## Series Overview

| Week | Topic | What We Actually Build |
|------|-------|----------------------|
| 1 | Foundation | Objects, fields, pipeline stages |
| 2 | Automation | Lead routing, scoring, workflows |
| 3 | Integration | Email, calendar, Slack, dashboards |
| 4 | Launch & Adoption | Training approach, adoption tracking |

---

## Episode 1: Foundation

**Title:** "Week 1: Building the CRM Foundation in Real-Time"

**Duration:** 60 minutes

### Agenda

**0-5 min: Intro**
- Why we're doing this series
- Who it's for (B2B tech, Series A-C)
- What we'll build over 4 weeks

**5-15 min: The Problem with Traditional Implementation**
- Why most take 6+ months
- The "discovery trap"
- Why speed actually improves outcomes

**15-45 min: Live Build — Foundation**
- Setting up a new HubSpot/Salesforce instance
- Creating pipeline stages (with rationale)
- Defining deal properties (only what you need)
- Setting permissions (keep it simple)
- Importing initial data

**45-55 min: Q&A**
- Live questions from audience

**55-60 min: Wrap + Next Week Preview**
- Homework: Audit your current pipeline stages
- Next week: Automation

### Key Talking Points

- "You don't need 47 pipeline stages. You need 5-7 that match reality."
- "Every custom field should answer: what decision does this help make?"
- "Permissions should be loose to start. Tighten later when you need to."

---

## Episode 2: Automation

**Title:** "Week 2: Lead Routing, Scoring, and Workflows"

**Duration:** 60 minutes

### Agenda

**0-5 min: Recap**
- What we built last week
- Questions from the community

**5-15 min: The Automation Philosophy**
- Automate the boring stuff
- Don't automate judgment calls
- The "would a human do this the same way every time?" test

**15-45 min: Live Build — Automation**
- Lead routing rules (round robin vs. territory)
- Lead scoring model (demo: simple 3-tier approach)
- Activity automation (auto-log, auto-update)
- Notification workflows (what deserves a Slack alert?)

**45-55 min: Q&A**

**55-60 min: Wrap + Next Week Preview**
- Homework: List your top 5 manual tasks reps hate doing
- Next week: Integration

### Key Talking Points

- "Lead scoring doesn't need to be complex. Tier 1/2/3 is enough to start."
- "If you're routing leads and it takes more than 5 minutes to explain the rules, simplify."
- "Slack alerts should be rare and high-signal. If everything is an alert, nothing is."

---

## Episode 3: Integration

**Title:** "Week 3: Connecting Email, Calendar, Slack, and Your Stack"

**Duration:** 60 minutes

### Agenda

**0-5 min: Recap**
- What we built last week
- Show automation in action

**5-15 min: Integration Philosophy**
- Native vs. third-party integrations
- The "single source of truth" principle
- When Zapier is fine vs. when you need native

**15-45 min: Live Build — Integration**
- Email integration setup
- Calendar sync
- Slack notifications (the right ones)
- Building a sales dashboard
- Connecting one key tool (e.g., LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Gong)

**45-55 min: Q&A**

**55-60 min: Wrap + Next Week Preview**
- Homework: List every tool your sales team uses daily
- Next week: Launch & Adoption

### Key Talking Points

- "Every integration is a potential point of failure. Connect what matters, not everything."
- "The dashboard should answer: Are we going to hit the number?"
- "If reps have to update two systems, they'll update zero systems."

---

## Episode 4: Launch & Adoption

**Title:** "Week 4: Training, Go-Live, and Getting Reps to Actually Use It"

**Duration:** 60 minutes

### Agenda

**0-5 min: Recap**
- Full tour of what we built in 3 weeks
- Before/after comparison

**5-15 min: Why Adoption Fails**
- The "go-live then abandon" problem
- Training for features vs. training for behavior
- The role of leadership accountability

**15-35 min: Live Build — Adoption Infrastructure**
- Creating an adoption dashboard (who's using it, who isn't)
- Setting up manager alerts (non-usage notifications)
- Quick wins: Mobile app setup, bookmarklets, shortcuts

**35-50 min: Training Best Practices**
- Live demo: How we run a training session
- The "show don't tell" approach
- Handling objections from reps

**50-55 min: Q&A**

**55-60 min: Series Wrap**
- Full summary of 4-week build
- Resources and templates
- How to work with LeanScale
