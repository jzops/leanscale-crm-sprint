# CRM Sprint — LinkedIn Paid Ads

## Campaign Structure

**Campaign Objective:** Lead Generation (or Website Visits → Retarget)
**Budget:** $2,000-5,000/month to start
**Audience:** See targeting below

---

## Audience Targeting

### Primary Audience: RevOps & Sales Leaders

**Job Titles (OR):**
- VP of Sales
- VP of Revenue
- Head of Revenue Operations
- Head of Sales Operations
- Director of RevOps
- CRO
- Chief Revenue Officer
- Director of Sales Operations

**Seniority:** Director, VP, CXO

**Company Size:** 51-1000 employees

**Industries:**
- Computer Software
- Information Technology
- Internet
- SaaS
- Financial Services (B2B only)

**Geography:** US, Canada, UK

---

### Secondary Audience: Founders & CEOs

**Job Titles:**
- CEO
- Founder
- Co-Founder
- Chief Executive Officer

**Company Size:** 11-200 employees

**Industries:** Same as above

**Additional Filter:** Recently raised funding (if available via LinkedIn or third-party data)

---

### Retargeting Audience

**Source:** Website visitors (landing page)
**Lookback:** 30 days
**Exclude:** People who already booked a call

---

## Ad Formats

### Format 1: Single Image Ad

**Best for:** Broad awareness, traffic to landing page

**Specs:**
- Image: 1200 x 627 px
- Headline: 70 characters max
- Description: 100 characters max
- CTA button: Learn More / Sign Up

---

### Format 2: Document Ad (Carousel)

**Best for:** Engagement, education, explaining the offer

**Specs:**
- 2-10 slides
- 1080 x 1080 px per slide (or 1080 x 1350 for vertical)
- Title text on each slide

---

### Format 3: Conversation Ad (Message Ad)

**Best for:** High-intent outreach, demo booking

**Specs:**
- Appears in inbox
- Multiple CTA buttons
- Lower volume, higher conversion

---

## A/B Test Plan

### Test 1: Headline Focus

**Version A:** Speed-focused ("30 Days, Not 6 Months")
**Version B:** Guarantee-focused ("Adoption Guaranteed or Money Back")
**Version C:** Price-focused ("$25K Flat Fee CRM Implementation")

**Metric:** CTR

---

### Test 2: Image Style

**Version A:** Abstract/minimal (timer icon, typography)
**Version B:** Dashboard screenshot (show the product)
**Version C:** Before/after visualization

**Metric:** CTR

---

### Test 3: CTA Button

**Version A:** "Learn More"
**Version B:** "Book Now"
**Version C:** "Get Started"

**Metric:** Conversion rate (clicks to landing page actions)

---

## Budget Allocation

**Month 1:**
- 60% Single image ads (broad awareness)
- 30% Document ads (engagement)
- 10% Conversation ads (test)

**Month 2+:**
- Shift budget toward best-performing format
- Add retargeting (20% of budget)
- Scale conversation ads if working
