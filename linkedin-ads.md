**Campaign Objective:** Lead Generation (or Website Visits → Retarget)
**Budget:** $2,000-5,000/month to start
**Audience:** See targeting below

## Ad Formats

### Format 1: Single Image Ad

**Best for:** Broad awareness, traffic to landing page

**Specs:**
- Image: 1200 x 627 px
- Headline: 70 characters max
- Description: 100 characters max
- CTA button: Learn More / Sign Up

---

### Format 2: Document Ad (Carousel)

**Best for:** Engagement, education, explaining the offer

**Specs:**
- 2-10 slides
- 1080 x 1080 px per slide (or 1080 x 1350 for vertical)
- Title text on each slide

---

### Format 3: Conversation Ad (Message Ad)

**Best for:** High-intent outreach, demo booking

**Specs:**
- Appears in inbox
- Multiple CTA buttons
- Lower volume, higher conversion

---

## Video Ad Creative

### Video Ad 1: "The 30-Second Sprint Pitch"

**Format:** Joe to camera, 30 seconds
**Structure:** Hook + Offer + CTA
**Script Flow:**
- **0-3s:** Hook — "Your CRM implementation shouldn't take 6 months."
- **3-15s:** Offer — Introduce the 30-Day Sprint: what it is, what's included, flat fee.
- **15-25s:** Proof — Mention clients, adoption guarantee.
- **25-30s:** CTA — "Book a 15-minute call. Link in comments."

---

### Video Ad 2: "Before & After"

**Format:** Screen recording, 60 seconds
**Structure:** Show a CRM transformation side by side
**Script Flow:**
- **0-3s:** Hook — "This is what most CRMs look like on Day 1. This is what ours look like on Day 30."
- **3-30s:** Before — Messy spreadsheets, empty pipeline, no dashboards.
- **30-55s:** After — Clean pipeline, automated routing, live dashboards, activity tracking.
- **55-60s:** CTA — "30 days. $25K. Link below."

---

### Video Ad 3: "Client Testimonial"

**Format:** Client talking about their experience, 45 seconds
**Structure:** Problem → Solution → Result
**Script Flow:**
- **0-3s:** Hook — Client states the problem they had.
- **3-20s:** Problem — What their CRM situation looked like before the sprint.
- **20-35s:** Solution — What the sprint experience was like, how fast it moved.
- **35-45s:** Result — Metrics, adoption, team reaction. CTA to learn more.

---

### Video Ad Specs

- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1350 (vertical)
- **Captions:** Required on all videos (85% of LinkedIn video is watched without sound)
- **First 3 seconds:** Must hook — no logos, no intros, straight to the point
- **Thumbnail:** Custom frame, not auto-generated

> **Note:** Video ads typically see 2-3x higher engagement on LinkedIn compared to static image ads. Prioritize these for retargeting and warm audiences.
