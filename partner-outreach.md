# CRM Sprint — Partner Outreach

## Partner Categories

### 1. HubSpot Solutions Partners
These are agencies/consultants already in the HubSpot ecosystem. Many don't do quick-turn implementation work.

### 2. Fractional CROs / VP Sales
Consultants who advise on sales strategy but don't do technical implementation.

### 3. VC Operating Partners
Portfolio support teams who need a go-to partner for CRM work.

### 4. Salesforce Partners Who Don't Do SMB
Enterprise-focused SI partners who get inbound from smaller companies.

## Referral Fee Structure

**Option A: Fixed Fee**
- $2,500 per closed deal
- Paid within 30 days of project kickoff

**Option B: Revenue Share**
- 10% of project value
- Paid within 30 days of final payment received

**Option C: Reciprocal**
- No cash exchange
- We refer work to each other when appropriate
- Track referrals quarterly

**Recommendation:** Start with Option C for strategic partners (VC operating partners, fractional CROs). Use Option A or B for transactional referrals.

## Partner Onboarding (Once Agreed)

1. **Intro call** (15 min) — Explain our process, answer questions
2. **Share one-pager** — PDF they can send to their clients
3. **Set up tracking** — How we'll track and attribute referrals
4. **Agree on fee structure** — Cash or reciprocal
5. **First referral** — We send them one to demonstrate value
