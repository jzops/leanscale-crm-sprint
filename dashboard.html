Core Message

Speed + Guarantee

Not competing on price or features. Competing on speed-to-value and risk elimination. "Consultants Talk. We Ship."

Why Now

91% of 11+ employee companies use CRM. Cloud CRM at 87%. Economic pressure = tighter timelines, smaller budgets. AI changing automation possibilities.
