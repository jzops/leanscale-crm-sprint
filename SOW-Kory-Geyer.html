Project Scope

Based on discovery, LeanScale will focus on three interconnected workstreams designed to build a foundation for scalable, signal-driven outbound and improved pipeline generation.

1. Account Data Validation & Cleanup

Audit and remediate account data quality within Salesforce, leveraging Clay for multi-provider validation. This includes resolving parent-child account relationships, identifying and correcting bad or redirect URLs, flagging duplicate or invalid accounts, and establishing a clean account foundation for downstream enrichment and targeting.

2. Contact Data Enrichment & Acquisition

Clean existing contact records and acquire net-new contacts for target personas at validated accounts. Leverage Clay's multi-provider waterfall approach (Cognism, Common Room, and additional Clay-integrated providers) to maximize coverage and accuracy post-ZoomInfo sunset.

3. MQA Model & Signal-Driven Prioritization

Design and implement a Marketing Qualified Account (MQA) model combining first-party signals (website activity, marketing campaigns, free assessment completions), partner propensity lists (Microsoft and channel partners), and third-party intent data (Common Room). Build prioritization logic that routes high-signal accounts into Gong Engage sequences for BDR and AE action, with clear SLAs and workflow automation.
