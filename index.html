FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before booking a sprint.

How is this so fast?

We've done 50+ implementations. We don't need 6 weeks of "discovery" to learn best practices — we already know them. Day 1 is build day, not kickoff meeting day.

What if our sales process is complex?

The 30-day sprint covers standard B2B sales process setup. Complex integrations, custom objects, or multi-team setups can be added — we scope it on the first call.

HubSpot or Salesforce?

Both. We're platform-agnostic and will recommend the right one based on your team size, sales process complexity, and budget. If you already have a preference, we run with it.

What does the guarantee actually mean?

For the Sprint: if reps aren't using the system by Day 30, we keep working at no extra cost. For the Guarantee Package: 80%+ daily active usage by Day 60 or a full refund. In writing.

How many sprints do you run at once?

We cap at 4 per month to maintain quality. Every sprint gets a dedicated architect — not someone juggling 10 projects. If we're booked, you're first on the waitlist.

What do we need to provide?

Admin access to your CRM (or we set one up), your current data exports, and 1-2 hours per week of your team's time for feedback, review, and training sessions.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Every package includes 30 days of Slack support post-launch. After that, we offer retainer options for ongoing optimization. But the goal is to hand you a self-sufficient system.

Can you help us choose a CRM?

Yes. We'll discuss this on the first call. Short version: HubSpot for teams under 50 reps, Salesforce for complex enterprise needs. Both work well for most B2B companies.
